About Me

I have always been a creative person. I love color, art, design, and decorating. For my entire life I have always gotten excited about the color swatch isle at the hardware store, rearranging furniture, and creating things. I am currently a Senior in the Visual Communication Design program, pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts at Eastern Washington University. Outside of design, I am also an outdoor enthusiast.
